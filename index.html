About UW 2DO

Helping University of Waterloo students stay organized and succeed academically

We understand how overwhelming university can be with multiple courses, assignments, and deadlines. UW 2DO was created to help University of Waterloo students take control of their academic schedule by automatically extracting and organizing important dates from course syllabi. Simply upload your course syllabus and our AI-powered system will analyze the document to identify assignments, exams, and their due dates. We'll highlight the most urgent tasks and help you prioritize your work effectively. This tool was designed specifically with University of Waterloo students in mind, understanding the unique challenges of managing course loads and deadlines in a fast-paced academic environment.
